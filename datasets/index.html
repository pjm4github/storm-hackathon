Gnip

Gnip is the world’s largest and most trusted provider of social media data, serving customers in a range of industries. Gnip’s customers deliver social media analytics to over 90% of the Fortune 500. Gnip delivers more than 100 billion realtime social data activities each month, providing access to data from dozens of sources including Twitter, Facebook, etc.
